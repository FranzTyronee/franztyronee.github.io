Limon B. Rodriguez
I am a doctoral candidate in international affairs with specialization in development economics at Johns Hopkins University School of Advanced International Studies (SAIS).

Previously, I worked at the United Nations (UN) in New York, notably, United Nations Development Programme Human Development Report Office and Bureau for Development Policy, UN Women, and UN Department of Economic and Social Affairs; UN Economic and Social Commission for Asia and the Pacific Trade Policy Section in Bangkok; Asian Development Bank Economics and Research Department in Manila; and a strategy consulting and digital technology company, Accenture, in Singapore and Manila.

I obtained my double masters in public policy and public administration with concentration in economic development policy from Columbia University School of International and Public Affairs (SIPA) and National University of Singapore Lee Kuan Yew School of Public Policy.
